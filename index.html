Baking for others shows you care. It can be an expression of love, appreciation, celebration, and even sympathy. Baking traditionally uses eggs, butter, and milk derived from animals, but it is possible to bake tasty treats without animal products. When we bake using whole plant based foods that are minimally processed we are showing compassion towards animals, the planet, and ourselves. Our personal food choices have an impact on many beings and I love recreating recipes from my childhood without the use of animal products.
